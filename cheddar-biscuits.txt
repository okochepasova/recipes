Ingredients:
 - 2 cups flour
 - 1 tbsp. baking powder
 - 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
 - 2 tbsp. chopped fresh herns (parsley, basil, or rosemary)
 - 1 cup 'Philadelphia' light cream cheese product
 - 1/2 cup milk
 - 1 egg, lightly beaten
 - 12 fresh flat leaf parsley leaves

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.

 2. Mix Flower and baking powder. Add cheese and herbs; stir untill combined. 
    Cut in cream cheese product with 2 knives or pastry blender to form crumbly 
	mixture. Stir in milk until mixture forms dough.

 3. Place dough on lightly flowered surface; kneed 10 times. Pat into 1/2-inch 
    thick circle. Cut with 2 inch cutter dusted with flour. Place on baking 
	sheet. Brush each lightly with beaten egg. Top each with 1 parsley leaf; 
	lightly brush again with egg.

 4. Bake 12 to 15 min. or until lightly browned. Serve warm.
